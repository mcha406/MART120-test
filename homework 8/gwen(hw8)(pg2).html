gwen's short story

the hunting lynx

her paws were silent beneath the freshly laid snow. she wandered carefully through the forest, ears perked and listening.

it's been about three days since her last meal and it wasn't as nearly a large of prey as she wanted it to be.

she crept carefully forward for a moment longer.

she paused, tilting her head downwards. she spots a large hare, digging into an area just below a tree stump.

all within one swift lift of her paws, her teeth sunk into the hare's throat. she exhaled through her nose effortlessly, dropping

her prey at her paws, watching her perfect sandy fur drip with the fresh blood of her next meal. this will surely be enjoyable.
